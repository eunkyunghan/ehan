CorepowerYoga Mobile App Redesign

Role: UXUI Designer

Goal: To redesign a mobile app

Timeline: 5 months, August - December 2024

Software: Figma

Problems

CorePowerYoga.com operated two separate mobile apps, but their visuals looked completely different, causing confusion for users.

Solution

A multitab element was introduced, enabling the two apps to merge into a singleunified experience.

Wireframe

The wireframe applied the multi-tab structure, merging the apps into a single tutorial platform designed to help users continue exercising at home.

UI Design

All visual elements were carefully controlled for consistency, reinforcing brand identity and improving usability.

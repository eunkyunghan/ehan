Brochure Design Project

Role: Graphic Designer

Target: Residents in the Bay Area

Timeline: 2 months, October - November 2025

Software: Adobe InDesign, Illustrator & Photoshop

Sketch

After carefully scanning ninlivesfoundation.org, I selected the information relevant to the cat clinic and regrouped it. Each category was arranged to distribute visual weight in a balanced layout.

Brand Identity

A brand logo was developed, serving as a strong design guide and visual anchor for the project.

Final Draft

Each panel was designed with consistent visuals, reinforcing brand identity while presenting key information to users.
